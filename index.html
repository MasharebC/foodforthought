food insecurity is a growing problem affecting millions of people worldwide, including those in our own communities. It refers to the lack of access to enough food for an active, healthy life due to financial, social, or other circumstances.




Quick Facts💯

🚨

1 in 8 people in the United States struggles with hunger.


👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Food insecurity is more common among families with children, people of color, and people living in rural areas.


🤕

Hunger can have a negative impact on a person's health, education, and economic opportunities.
